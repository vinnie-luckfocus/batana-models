# Task: 2D Visualization Video Generation

## Description
Build an OpenCV-based renderer that produces a mandatory side-by-side comparison video for every corrected video. Left pane shows the original preprocessed video with raw RTMPose skeleton in red; right pane shows the same video with corrected skeleton in green and corrected keypoints highlighted in yellow. Overlay filename and frame number at the top. Output 1920x1080 @ 30 fps videos to `visuals/2d/train/` and `visuals/2d/val/`.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Renderer script processes every usable corrected video; videos marked `UNREFINABLE` after correction are explicitly skipped from rendering
- [ ] Side-by-side output is 1920x1080, 30 fps, 120 frames
- [ ] Left pane: raw skeleton in red using COCO skeleton + bat line
- [ ] Right pane: corrected skeleton in green with corrected keypoints highlighted in yellow
- [ ] Top overlay displays `video_id` and `Frame: N/120`
- [ ] All outputs saved to `visuals/2d/train/` and `visuals/2d/val/`
